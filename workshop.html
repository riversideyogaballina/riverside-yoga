What this is

Get the foundations right,
finally.

Yoga Foundations is our monthly workshop series at Riverside Yoga Ballina, covering the essential building blocks of vinyasa yoga. Each 90-minute session is instructive and welcoming, for anyone who's new to yoga, returning after a long gap, or just wants to feel more grounded in their practice.


It's fun, relaxed, with plenty of breathing room and the perfect chance to meet the community.

What you'll walk away with

More than just a class.

Foundations that stick

Learn the poses that underpin every class, done properly. No guessing, no confusion.

Confidence in your body

Feel at ease in a yoga class, maybe for the first time. This is what beginners actually need.

Community from day one

Meet people who are exactly where you are. No intimidating yogis, no comparing.

    Your teachers

    You'll be in good hands.

    Selin

    Co-founder & Teacher

    Selin is a yoga teacher and hypnotherapist with over a decade of practice, trained by some of the world's most respected teachers across India, Turkey, the US and Australia. She brings a rare blend of intelligent movement, meditative awareness and deep presence to every class. Her flows are both uplifting and calming, and she has a gift for making every student feel truly seen. Through her hypnotherapy practice and yoga teaching, Selin's passion is inner transformation: helping people find stillness, grounding and a genuine connection to themselves.

    Collette

    Co-founder & Teacher

    Collette Keehnen is a 200-hour certified yoga teacher with over 15 years of practice and a Northern Rivers local for the past decade. Shaped by years of travel and a deep love of yoga philosophy, Collette's approach is grounded and unhurried. She has a special interest in helping people take what they learn on the mat and actually apply it to their lives, building a quiet confidence that extends well beyond the class. She started teaching yoga with one simple intention: to create a space where everyone feels welcome and yoga is accessible to all.
